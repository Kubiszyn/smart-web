InPOST


InPost is a logistic operator of a network of parcel machines that enable independent sending and collection of parcels 24 hours a day, in a location chosen by the customer. The devices are located in the vicinity of housing estates and shops and are open 24/7. As of February 11, 2021, the operator has a network of 13 thousand. parcel machines all over Poland.

DHL


DHL consists of four divisions: Express, Global Forwarding and Freight, Supply Chain and Global Mail. The company's headquarters are located in Bonn, Germany. DHL Express is a world leader in international express services. The company's network covers more than 220 countries. DHL Express provides courier and express services to both companies and individual clients. The company employs over 100,000. employees worldwide and serves over 8 million customers.

DPD


DPD is a courier company operating on the international market. The company's mission is to be competent, friendly and close to the client. The company also focuses on sustainable development, caring for the natural environment, its employees and noting current social problems. The company boasts a well-functioning European network and professional local partners. During 2010, the company delivered 34 million parcels in the country. DPD employs approximately 5,000 people in Poland.
